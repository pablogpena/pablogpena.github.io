About Me

I am a PhD researcher at the Real Jardín Botánico (RJB-CSIC), where I develop novel artificial intelligence methods to model complex evolutionary and diversification scenarios. My research combines computational innovation with empirical data collection, bridging theory and observation to better understand how species diversify and distribute across time and space. I enjoy tackling a wide range of evolutionary biology questions, applying my programming skills to find practical solutions across different systems and organisms.
